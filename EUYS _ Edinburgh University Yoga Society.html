Home

You can now sign up for your 2015/2016 membership!

Welcome to Edinburgh University Yoga Society’s website.

As a committee we aim to make yoga as inexpensive and accessible as we can for our members (both students and non-students). We offer a variety of classes and workshops in a variety of locations in Edinburgh throughout the year – see our timetable page for more information!

If you have a question (and it’s not answered on the FAQ page) or would like to get involved (we always need more help!) then please contact us.

Namaste.
